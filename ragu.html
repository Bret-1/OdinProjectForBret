Odin Project

Ragu

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, carrot and celary
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Milk and chicken stock
  • Herbs

Start by getting a tall pot very hot, and adding some olive oil followed by the ground meat. Using a wooden spoon,
break apart the meat. After a couple minutes, add the onion, carrot and celary. Scrape the bottom of the pan as these
release moisture. When softened, add the garlic followed by the tomato paste after a minute. Saute for another minute
then add the tomato sauce, herbs, milk and stock. Bring to a simmer and cook uncovered for 2 hours.
