Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ragu
  • Bechamel
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

First, add a warm layer of your ragu or meat sauce of choice to the bottom of a 13 x 9 baking tin.
Follow with a layer of noodles. Next add layers of ragu, bechamel, and both cheeses repeating till you
run out of noodles. The top layer should have less ragu, but more bechamel and mozzarella.
